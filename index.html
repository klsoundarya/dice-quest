Dice Quest

Game Rules

  1. Each player takes turns rolling two dice.
  2. The players can decide who goes first by rolling one die each, with the highest roll starting the game.
  3. If the player rolls a double (both dice show the same number), they get to roll again, the sum of the numbers rolled is added to the player's current score.
    • Example: If a player rolls a double , their current turn's score is reset to 0, and their turn ends.
  4. If a player rolls any other double (1-1, 2-2, 3-3, 4-4, or 5-5), they get to roll again and add the new roll to their current score.
  5. The player can choose to "hold" and not roll again, which means their current score is added to their total score, and their turn ends.
  6. Restart: Click "Reset" to start the game over anytime.
