Dice Quest

Game Rules

  1. Each player takes turns to roll the dice.
  2. There are total 10 rounds, player with the higher roll wins the round.
  3. If both players roll the same number, it's a draw.
  4. Click "Roll Dice" to roll the dice and see the results.
  5. Click "Edit Names" to enter new player names.
  6. By clicking "Roll Dice" button, the score will be updated from 0
  7. Restart: Click the "Start New Game" button to begin a new game with a fresh set of players.
